InAlley
Guide Service For Tainan Alleys

Product service system designed for tourist to explore Tainan Middle-West area

OVERVIEW
About the Project

In this project, we present “InAlley” a product-service system that solves some of the major problems faced by foreign tourists by integrating existing technology and service to help tourists explore Tainan alleys that are not marked on the map, and take a look of local stores and sights. InAlley can organize the number of attractions into itinerary and plan the most efficient route. Combined with iBeacons in alleys, tourists can get information about stores, attractions, graffiti when they walkthrough.

Timeline: 2 months

My Role: UX researcher, UX designer, UI designer

THE CHALLENGE

Tainan is an ancient city with many monuments and old buildings that are hidden in alleys and aren't marked on the map. The challenge is to design a product-service system that breaks the barrier of language and makes foreign tourists more willing and easier to step into the unknown lane, explore Tainan features together.

USER RESEARCH
Field Research

We conducted field research to understand tourists’ behavior when traveling in Tainan. Four of us took a trip to the Mid-east area of Tainan and observed foreign tourists directly. We recorded their behavior and interaction. After the observation, we ask our target groups to do a short semi-structured interview.

Observation

We took the observation furtively and find the interaction pattern of foreign tourists. We recorded the data with pictures and notes. For the sake of personal privacy, we prevented taking pictures of their faces.

Interviews

1. How do you feel about the tour?
2. What information you want to know when sightseeing?
How do you get the information about tourist sights?
4. What tools do you usually use when traveling?

RESEARCH INSIGHTS
Breakdowns

Main Problems

1. Device only shows main road, not alleys.

2. Does not know what is inside the alleys.

3. Alleys are little known and not obvious.

Minor problem

1. Get lost.

2. Hard to plan route.

EX. City map: too much pen marks make it messy.

EX. Google map: can only use when having internet.

TEH SOLUTION
Signboard + Guide App

We designed “InAlley” service system which contains app and sightseeing boards. Visitors can see the introduction and download the app through the sightseeing boards nearby roads. Travelers can use the app to explore the alley and get the stores’ or attractions’ information and choose where to go. “Inalley” can organize attractions together and create the best tour route. In addition, the scenic spots inside the alley are fitted with iBeacon, when the visitor passes by, their mobile will receive the store information or the scenic spot introduction. Tourists can choose to visit freely or not.

Signboard

1. Established in front of the alleys.
2. Show the map of this area.
3. Mark restaurants, stores, monuments, graffis on the map.
4. Have a QR code that provides free wifi.

Guide App

1. Integrated with iBeacons which transmits data to tourists’ phones as they pass.
2. Get information about sightseeing.
3. Customize the tour routes.
